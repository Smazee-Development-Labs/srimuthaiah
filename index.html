About Us

"Registered under Indian Trust Act 1982, Sri Muthaiah Memorial Trust has been founded with the sole intention to facilitate the under-privileged communities to get high quality employment oriented training. The founders of this trust come from reputed institutions with over 30 years of experience in education and business management and dedicatedly work for the noble cause of helping the underserved sections of society. Sri Muthaiah Memorial Trust has set up large scale education and training infrastructure devoted to train the economically backward communities across India, especially in Tamil Nadu. The Trust has a dedicated team to train and transform the students for the jobs that they desire to do. This not only helps them to access decent employment, but also makes them respectable representatives of the society. This Trust has a strong strategic emphasis on youth employability."



Sri Muthaiah Memorial Trust has launched a number of initiatives which help in enhancing young people's access to market driven vocational skills. Through these initiatives, SMMT aims to achieve decent employment for youth living in poor urban and rural areas and ensure that all new entrants to workforce, including young girls, are equipped with the knowledge and skills needed for high productivity and high quality jobs. Vocational Training is provided in Fashion Technology, Catering, Health Assistant, Electrician and AC Refrigeration Mechanics for the youths through Sri Muthaih Memorial Trust Community College and ITES training is provided through Sri Muthaih Memorial Trust of Computer Education.



Sri Muthaiah Memorial Trust is not limited to educating people but has also conducted several programs for public awareness such as Road Safety Programs, HIV & Cancer Awareness Programs, Dental & Eye Camps in rural areas, Health care awareness program in Siddha, Awareness of Election to the Voters, and Placement campaign for unemployed youths and EDP Trainings for Self help Groups.




VISION

  • To be and end-to-end community based solutions provider in the areas of education,
    health and sustainable livelihood for vulnerable communities.

  • To enhance the Socio-economic status of young underprivileged women by empowering them with self confidence
    and required skills to enable them to become independent and contribute to family and society.

  • Identifying, Creating & Implementing Research Led Industry oriented curriculum, leading to employment.
    We strongly believe that quality of education depends not only on the orientation of its courses
    to industry standards and quality, but also on quality of faculty.

  • Continuous skill up gradation, interactive seminars and workshop,exposure to newer technologies are some
    new measures that are being introduced shortly to enhance the skills of knowledge up gradation group.


MISSION

  • To provide low-literate or illiterate youth with the power to earn a regular and decent livelihood.

  • To empower the underprivileged women, children, elderly and physically challenged individuals by enriching their lives through education, health and sustainable livelihood skills and opportunities.

  • Research led, employer preferred constantly updated training program.

  • National and global presence by establishing networks.

  • Bridging the gap between industry and the unemployed youth.
